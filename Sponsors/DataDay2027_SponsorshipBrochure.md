## About MORPC and Data Day

The Mid-Ohio Regional Planning Commission (MORPC) is Central Ohio's voice, serving more than 95 local governments and community partners across a 15-county region. Through its Regional Data Roundtable (RDR), a body of top-level advisory experts from the public and private sectors, MORPC works to make Central Ohio a leader in using data to improve the quality of life for all residents.

Data Day is the RDR's annual one-day event, where attendees learn how the region is promoting data-driven decision-making and how communities use, secure, govern, and visualize data. Data Day 2027, themed *Plotting What's Next*, marks the fifth annual gathering, a milestone year and a strong stage for your organization alongside a trusted MORPC signature event.

## Why sponsor

Sponsoring Data Day puts your organization in front of the people who create, analyze, and use data across Central Ohio, and backs a community that has grown for five years.

- **A targeted audience.** Roughly 200 data leaders, practitioners, and analysts from local government, nonprofits, education, and civic-minded business, together in one room for a full day.
- **Fifth-anniversary visibility.** Year five brings heightened attention across the region. Your logo appears on event promotional material, and higher tiers put your team on the plenary stage and in front of every attendee.
- **Direct engagement.** Host or moderate a breakout session, staff an exhibitor table, and connect face-to-face with prospective partners, clients, and talent throughout the day.
- **Support the next generation.** Sponsor scholarships that bring college students into the regional data community and into conversation with your organization.
- **Back the region's data future.** Your support keeps Data Day accessible and helps convene the partnerships and shared learning that move data practice forward across Central Ohio.
