This is a no code repository. Context and working state for the Data Day 2027 conference, with a current focus on developing the program and materials for planning. Read this first before working in the repo.

## Project overview

**The event.** Data Day is a single-day conference convening Central Ohio's public-sector data community — data users, practitioners, and analysts across local governments, municipal officials, contractors, nonprofits, educational institutions, and civic-minded businesses. It offers a wide range of breakout sessions across skill levels and topics.

**The client.** Mid-Ohio Regional Planning Commission (MORPC), a regional council of governments and metropolitan planning organization covering 95+ Central Ohio local governments across a 15-county region. MORPC convenes the **Regional Data Roundtable (RDR)**, an advisory board that focuses on regional data topics and hosts Data Day annually.

**Data Day 2027** will be the **5th annual** event (2026 was the 4th). Treat the 5th-anniversary milestone as a narrative anchor across program, marketing, and sponsorship.

### Quick facts
- **Date:** Tuesday, February 16, 2027.
- **Venue:** Ohio State University, Vitria on the Square, 14 E. 15th Ave., Columbus, OH 43201 (selected; booking pending). Note: this is a new venue for 2027 — the 2026 event was at Columbus State Community College Workforce Development Center.
- **Format:** ~8:30 AM–4:00 PM; morning keynote, breakout sessions, lunch keynote, more breakouts. Sessions ~45 min with significant Q&A/engagement.
- **Planning cycle:** ~13 months. The 2026 cycle ran Jan 2025 → Feb 2026; by that cadence the 2027 cycle began ~Jan 2026 and is ~5 months in as of mid-2026.

## Source materials (in project files)
- **Data_Day_2026_Agenda_DRAFT.pdf** — full 2026 program. Note: nearly every breakout lists a *Facilitator* (relevant — facilitation is a natural Champion day-of role). Signals AI/data leadership, GIS, digital twins, transit, housing, program evaluation as recurring topics.
- **DataDay2026_CFP.pdf** — 2026 Call for Proposals. Theme: *People + Data = Inspired Futures*. Preference given to (1) GIS in planning/decision-making and (2) local social-science research or government/nonprofit program evaluation. Defines submission fields (title, objectives, description, abstract, speakers, skill level, audience, engagement).
- **FINAL_DRAFT_20262028_REGIONAL_DATA_AGENDA.pdf** — RDR's governing agenda. **Primary voice/style reference for outward MORPC/RDR communications.** Four focus areas (Transportation & Mobility; Housing & Community Development; Infrastructure & Economic Development; Energy & Sustainability) and four action pillars (Promote Reliable & Trusted Data; Expand Education & Outreach; Bolster Project & Program Support; Increase Representation & Connection). Data Day is named a "Signature Event." Note: Pillar 4 uses the word "champion," which the Champions program intentionally echoes.
- **master_timeline.xlsx** — the planning timeline. Sheets: *Master Timeline* (Category, Task, Notes, Date Due, Hours, Owner), *Time Allocations* (~208 total hours, heavily "Data" team), *Task Categories*. Dates are Excel serials; the event task = serial 46063 = Feb 10, 2026, so the 2027 cycle is the same structure shifted ~1 year. **In 2027 the monthly Working Group is being replaced by the Champions model.**
