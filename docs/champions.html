Five ways to help.

Take on as many or as few as you like. For every one, we provide the templates, links, and talking points — so it's genuinely quick.

1 · Connect us with keynote speakers

Surface names and open doors to speakers who fit the theme. You suggest and introduce; staff invite and negotiate.

Aug–Oct · about an hour

2 · Share the Call for Proposals

Spread the CFP through your channels and personally nudge two or three strong potential presenters to submit.

Late Sep–early Nov · an hour or two

3 · Help solicit sponsors

Suggest organizations that might sponsor and make warm introductions. Staff own the package, pricing, and the ask.

Sep–Nov · as little as one intro

4 · Encourage registration

Once registration opens, invite your network and colleagues — and point people to the sessions they'd value.

Nov–late Jan · an hour, spread out

5 · Take a day-of role

Facilitate a breakout (the anchor role), assist a workshop, greet attendees, or capture the day in photos.

Assigned in January · event day

How it works.

You connect, amplify, and host

Suggest a keynote, share the Call for Proposals, open a door to a sponsor, or lend a hand on event day.

MORPC owns the rest

Staff make the decisions, handle money and commitments, and manage every invitation and logistic.

You'll always have what you need

Every ask comes with a ready-made kit: templates, links, graphics, and talking points.

Ready to help?
