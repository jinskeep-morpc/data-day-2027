What it is

A single day of breakout sessions across skill levels and topics, from GIS and program evaluation to responsible AI in the public sector, plus morning and lunch keynotes.

Who attends

Data users, practitioners, analysts, and decision-makers from local government, nonprofits, education, and civic-minded business.

Who convenes it

The Mid-Ohio Regional Planning Commission (MORPC) and its Regional Data Roundtable, serving 95+ local governments across a 15-county region.
