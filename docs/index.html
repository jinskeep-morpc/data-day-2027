Notify me About Data Day

Five years in, the pattern is clear.

For five years, Data Day has convened Central Ohio's data community around a shared belief — that better use of data improves the region. Each year added another point to the plot. Now we read the trend line and chart what comes next.

What it is

A single day of breakout sessions across skill levels and topics — from GIS and program evaluation to responsible AI in the public sector — plus morning and lunch keynotes.

Who attends

Data users, practitioners, and analysts across local governments, municipal officials, contractors, nonprofits, educational institutions, and civic-minded businesses.

Who convenes it

The Mid-Ohio Regional Planning Commission (MORPC) and its Regional Data Roundtable, serving 95+ local governments across a 15-county region.
