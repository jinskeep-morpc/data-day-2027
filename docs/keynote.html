What makes a strong Data Day keynote.

The best keynotes speak to the whole room — practitioners and leaders across local governments, nonprofits, education, and civic-minded business.

Forward-looking

Speaks to where public-sector data use and analysis are heading — a look ahead, not a retrospective.

Relevant to the region

Resonates with Central Ohio's public sector — the work of local governments and the communities they serve.

Draws a crowd

A compelling voice attendees will make time for. Someone whose perspective sets the tone for the day.

Engaging & accessible

Substance that lands across skill levels, with room for the Q&A and discussion Data Day is known for.
