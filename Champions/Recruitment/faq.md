# Data Day 2027 Champions — Frequently Asked Questions

## About the role

**What is a Data Day Champion?**
A Champion is a member of Central Ohio's data community who helps make Data Day happen — by connecting us with speakers and sponsors, spreading the word, and lending a hand on event day. You help at a few key moments through the year, in the ways that fit you best.

**Is this a committee? Are there meetings?**
No. There's no standing committee and no recurring meetings. Each way to help is a specific, time-bound ask. We reach out at the right moments, you take on what works for you, and that's it.

**Why be a Champion?**
You'll help shape the region's premier data event, grow your network across local governments, nonprofits, educational institutions, and businesses, and get an inside look at the work happening across Central Ohio. It's also a meaningful way to give back to the data community in our fifth-anniversary year.

---

## Time and commitment

**How much time does this really take?**
Most individual asks take an hour or two, and several can be done in a few minutes (forwarding the Call for Proposals, making an introduction). You decide how many you take on, so the total is entirely up to you.

**Do I have to help with everything?**
Not at all. There are five ways to help, and you opt into the ones that interest you. Picking a single ask is genuinely welcome.

**When does this start and end?**
Asks are spread across the planning year — speaker and sponsor connections in the fall, promotion and registration through the winter, and event-day roles in February. Each one comes with clear timing so nothing sneaks up on you. After the event on February 16, the commitment is complete.

**What if I sign up and then can't follow through?**
That's completely fine. These are voluntary, low-pressure asks. If something comes up, just let us know and we'll cover it. We'd rather you take on only what you can comfortably do.

---

## Who can help

**Do I need to be a data expert or technically skilled?**
No. Champions come from all backgrounds and skill levels. What helps most is a genuine interest in the regional data community and a willingness to share it with your network.

**Who can be a Champion?**
Anyone connected to Central Ohio's data community is welcome — people in local government, nonprofits, educational institutions, and civic-minded businesses, including past Data Day speakers and attendees.

---

## The asks themselves

**Will I have to ask anyone for money?**
No. If you help with sponsorship, your role is simply to suggest organizations and make introductions. MORPC staff handle the package, the pricing, and the actual ask — you never quote prices or negotiate.

**Will I be cold-emailing strangers?**
No. We only ask you to reach out to people you already know, and always after checking that they're open to it. For broader promotion, we provide ready-made copy you can simply share.

**What if my own organization might be a speaker or sponsor?**
Wonderful — just hand it to our staff lead and we'll take it from there. That keeps things simple and avoids any conflict of interest for you.

**What if I'm not sure how to do a task?**
Every ask comes with what you need — templates, links, talking points, and a staff contact. If you're ever unsure, just reach out and we'll walk you through it.
