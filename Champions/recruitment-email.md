# Data Day Champions Recruitment Email

## Subject

Calling all Data Day Champion

## Body

Dear ...,

MORPC is looking for people to champion our signature Data Day conference. 

Do you want to be a part of making Data Day another great success?

Become a Data Day champion! We are asking that individuals commit to support and promoting Data Day throughout the year in 5 major areas:

1. Help recruit individuals who may be interested in present a breakout session by distributing and promoting the CFP. 
2. Connect MORPC with organizations and businesses who could sponsor Data Day.
3. Recommend and connect MORPC with exciting keynote speakers.
4. Share and promote registration and attendance. 
5. Boost the impacts of the event through reporting back on how you benefited from Data Day.

MORPC will provide you with timelines, information, and materials need for each task as needed.

All you need to do is respond positively to this email and we will add you to the Data Day Champion List then look out for your first email regarding keynote speakers in early July. 

Data Day could not be the region leading data event if it wasn't for your support. 

Thank you,

Jordan Inskeep and the MORPC Team
