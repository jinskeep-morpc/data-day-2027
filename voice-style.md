# Voice & Style — Data Day 2027

Three distinct audiences with distinct voices. Keep them in separate files; never mix audiences in one document.

---

## Outward MORPC/RDR communications
*(Recruitment emails, public-facing copy)*

Match the Regional Data Agenda voice — mission-forward, institutional, parallel structure. Name the four constituencies: local governments, nonprofits, educational institutions, civic-minded businesses. Preferred verbs: *leverage, foster, advance, convene*. Anchor on the RDR vision: "make Central Ohio a leader in using data to improve the quality of life for all residents."

Style reference: `FINAL_DRAFT_20262028_REGIONAL_DATA_AGENDA.pdf`

## Champion-facing materials
*(Handout, FAQ)*

Warm, clear, practical, second person ("you"). Mission-aware but readable. Lead with reassurance about lightness and low commitment.

## Internal/staff materials
*(Checklist, trackers)*

Direct, operational.

---

## Format default

Markdown unless a designed/branded output (one-pager, slide, brochure) or a spreadsheet is explicitly requested.
